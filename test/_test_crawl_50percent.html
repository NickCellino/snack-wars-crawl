PART I

THE SNACK WARS

For generations, the Goldfish Kingdom and the Pretzel Kingdom maintained an uneasy peace, each ruling their own domains within the vast Snack Kingdoms.

The Goldfish King, lord of the salty realms, commands loyalty from his knights, the fearsome Reese's Pumpkins. Across the border, the Pretzel King rules the sweet territories, his power bolstered by legions of Cookie warriors.

Though tensions have simmered for years, war was never inevitable—until now.

The Goldfish King has issued a formal declaration of war against the Pretzel King. Both monarchs now race to gather their armies for the inevitable conflict that will decide the fate of the Snack Kingdoms.

The war begins....
