PART III
THE COOKIE CRUMBLES

The conflict reaches new heights as betrayal strikes both kingdoms from within.

A secret alliance forms between the forgotten snacks - the forgotten pretzels and abandoned goldfish join forces against their former kings. The cookie armies fracture as loyalty is tested.

The Goldfish King discovers the truth about the tape realm's power, while the Pretzel King faces a rebellion among his sweetest warriors. Trust crumbles like stale cookies left in the open air.

In the shadows, a third kingdom watches and waits. The Vegetable Empire, long silent, begins to mobilize its forces of broccoli and carrot sticks.

The snack world will never be the same....
